### 发现2: 学习日志系统完整 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**日志分类**:
- **LEARNINGS.md**: 学习、修正、知识差距、最佳实践
- **ERRORS.md**: 错误日志、命令失败、异常、意外输出
- **FEATURE_REQUESTS.md**: 功能请求、用户需求、缺失能力

**日志格式**:
```markdown
## [LRN-YYYYMMDD-XXX] category

**Logged**: ISO-8601 timestamp
**Priority**: low | medium | high | critical
**Status**: pending
**Area**: frontend | backend | infra | tests | docs | config

### Summary
One-line description of what was learned

### Details
Full context: what happened, what was wrong, what's correct

### Suggested Action
Specific fix or improvement to make

### Metadata
- Source: conversation | error | user_feedback
- Related Files: path/to/file.ext
- Tags: tag1, tag2
- See Also: LRN-20250110-001 (if related to existing entry)
- Pattern-Key: simplify.dead_code | harden.input_validation
- Recurrence-Count: 1
- First-Seen: 2025-01-15
- Last-Seen: 2025-01-15

---
```

**ID 生成规则**:
- TYPE: `LRN` (学习), `ERR` (错误), `FEAT` (功能)
- YYYYMMDD: 当前日期
- XXX: 序号或随机3字符

**示例**: `LRN-20250115-001`, `ERR-20250115-A3F`, `FEAT-20250115-002`

### 发现4: 重复模式检测和简化与加固反馈 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**简化与加固反馈流程**:

3. **搜索**: 搜索 `.learnings/LEARNINGS.md` 是否存在该键的条目

   - 添加 `See Also` 链接到相关条目/任务
5. **如果未找到**:
   - 创建新的 `LRN-...` 条目
   - 设置 `Source: simplify-and-harden`
   - 设置 `Pattern-Key`, `Recurrence-Count: 1`, 和 `First-Seen`/`Last-Seen`
